Summer 2017 Chromebook Collection Details
- Please remove any stickers prior to turning in your Chromebook.
- Returning students will get same Chromebook in August.

Chromebook Turn In Schedule
Chromebooks, chargers & cases will be collected during homeroom on the dates below.
WMS - 6th & 7th - Tuesday, May 23rd (homeroom)
WMS - 8th - Tuesday, May 16th (homeroom)
WHS - 9th, 10th & 11th - May 22, 23 & 24 (one homeroom immediately follows each final exam)

WHS - Seniors - Wednesday, May 10th (homeroom) (Seniors requiring exams won't need Chromebooks.)
WHS - Seniors - Early Turn In Option- Mon. May 8 & Tues. May 9 (Post secondary, etc.) (WHS Tech Offices)
- Any fines must be paid prior to receiving cap and gown on Friday.
- Access to your Woodridge Google account will be disabled June 1st.
- To transfer/save your files, place them into one Google Drive folder and share it with a personal Google account.
- We wish you the best in your future endeavors. Once a Bulldog, always a Bulldog.
